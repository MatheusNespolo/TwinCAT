        Plastics meets packaging

        Plastics and packaging machines often operate as a team in the food, beverage, pharmaceutical, and cosmentics industries. This results in synergy that you can exploit using our cross-industry automation platform and the expertise of our industry specialists.

        Learn more

        The MX-System series, episode 3: The baseplates

        In the third episode of this video series, Paula Göbel, MX-System product manager, introduces the baseplates. They are the back wall of the system and form the foundation of control cabinet-free automation with the MX-System. In this episode, you can discover the differences between the baseplates and their common functions.

        Learn more

        Efficient battery production needs PC-based control technology

        From electrode manufacturing to module and pack assembly, you can control all processes from a single platform with our automation solutions. Find out more about practical implementation of our products and the advantages that it brings.

        Ajinomoto Omnichem is known for producing small molecules with complex chemistry and high potency, as well as for processes based on continuous flow processes. Together with Beckhoff, they have used the Module Type Package concept to automate their system.
